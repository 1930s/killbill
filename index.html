More than Subscription Billing

Kill Bill is an open source software tool that goes beyond subscription management. Its open architecture allows you to add plug-ins to capture all your billing logic. Other Subscription Billing solutions are limited to what you can configure through their UIs. Custom promotions, special offers, smart dunnings become too difficult to implement and your pricing model looks the same as your competitors. Kill Bill's plugin architecture means that custom logic is easy to add to give you the flexibility to create the pricing and promotions you need.

Kill Bill is running in production today at Ning

Open Source

Because it is open source software, you can see exactly what the system does. You can even download the system and run it on your laptop. You and every other Kill Bill user can check it for bugs and security flaws.

Open Data

Your customer's data belongs to you. With Kill Bill it is easy for you to access that data. Our API's let you export the data so that you can load up your entire customer data onto a test system.

Open Architecture

Extend your billing solution by creating Kill Bill Plugins to:

  1. Add custom billing logic
  2. Add custom analytics and reporting
  3. Integrate with other systems: CRM, ERP, BI
