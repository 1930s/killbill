Subscription Billing made easy

Adding a payment solution to your website should be easy: capture the credit card information via a third-party payment gateway and issue an API call for a given amount. Recurring billing is similar: do and repeat every month. Want a trial period of 30 days? Delay the initial payment. But now you want to add a second offering with a different price point. And a different trial period length. And support multiple currencies. And create rules to control upgrades and downgrades...

We believe billing should be easy. Killbill is an open-source (Apache License 2.0) billing platform which solves these problems and much more.

Authoritative

Killbill has the full billing picture: it knows about accounts, subscriptions, invoices, payments, overdue states, ... You have the whole picture of your business.

Customizable

Killbill is rules driven, meaning you can configure pretty much everything, from plans and international prices, to invoice layouts and payment failures emails.

Pluggable

Custom payment gateway? Complex enterprise integration? Extend the functionality of Killbill by writing your own plugin. Plugins can be as easy as a few lines of Ruby or as complex as OSGI bundles.
